# Social Media Do's and Don’ts Checklist

## Do's
- Use strong and unique passwords for your accounts.
- Think before posting content on online platforms and ensure your content is appropriate.
- Maintain a professional and respectful tone online.
- Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation.
- Adjust privacy settings to control who can view your content.

## Don’ts
- Do not share personal or sensitive information publicly.
- Avoid posting offensive, abusive, or inappropriate content.
- Do not click on suspicious links or unknown messages.
- Avoid oversharing details about your daily life .
- Do not engage in online arguments or cyberbullying.
