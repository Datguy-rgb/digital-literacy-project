# Stay Safe Online – Cybercrime Prevention Checklist

##  General Safety Tips
- Use strong and unique passwords for all your accounts.
- Never share your OTP, passwords, or PIN with anyone.
- Always verify the sender before clicking on links in emails or messages.
- Avoid downloading files or apps from unknown or untrusted sources.
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible.
- Keep your devices and apps updated to avoid security vulnerabilities.

##  UPI & Financial Safety Tips
- Do not approve unknown payment requests on UPI apps.
- Always double-check the receiver’s details before sending money.
- Never scan QR codes from unknown or suspicious sources.
- Do not share your UPI PIN with anyone under any circumstances.

##  Reporting Cybercrime
- Report cybercrime incidents at: https://cybercrime.gov.in
- Helpline Number: 1930 (24x7)
